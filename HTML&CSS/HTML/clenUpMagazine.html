Food and Travel Reader Awards 2020

Nine years ago we set out to create a unique series of awards. We wanted to impartially identify the very best in the business, judged by experts who could draw on real-life experiences to inform their vote. These experts had to be people with unrivalled knowledge and passion for travel and food. They had to be people like you. Unlike other industry awards, ours are entirely nominated and voted on by Food and Travel magazine readers.
